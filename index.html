Who We Are

Built to Last

Yorke & Toni Groups is a Ghana-based technology and fintech company operating at the intersection of software engineering, financial technology, and intelligent automation.

We have one long-term mission: to become Ghana's largest fintech company — and to reshape financial infrastructure across Africa. We're not building to sell. We're building to own.

Every product in our portfolio is chosen because it compounds. ALPHA generates financial performance. SCOUT owns a data layer in a massive market. StudyBuddy captures a generation of students before any competitor does. These aren't bets — they're positions.

01
Total Ownership
Core systems are built internally. Every algorithm, every platform, every data asset belongs to YT Groups — and appreciates accordingly.
02
Compounding Architecture
Each product builds a moat. Data from SCOUT feeds ALPHA. StudyBuddy's users become the next generation of fintech customers. The flywheel is intentional.
03
Africa-First Scale
Designed for mass adoption in African markets — with infrastructure built to grow from hundreds to millions without architectural debt.
